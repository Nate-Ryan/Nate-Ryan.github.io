About This Project

Engineering student council came to me with a new project. They wanted to make cookie cutters for our school logo "Zippy" a kangaroo.

To acomplish this project I implemented skills I gained from my poker chip models, that being the use of svg drawings. I converted a logo of zippy into a vector file and then imported the drawing into Solidworks. This was the bulk of the work considering cookie cutters are mostly an outline for the modeling side of things.

The added step which was new for me was epoxy dipping all of the parts in a food safe resin. There are many health dangers in 3d printed parts when not completely covered / made correctly. Even still they are not the best for long repeteated use and will typically not be dish washer safe.

I was happy to be able to meet the need of an organization that I am apart of, and provide a product that was sure to put a smile on many students face as they were handed out during finals week.
